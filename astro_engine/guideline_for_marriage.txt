        How to use these together

The logic should be:

Dasha rule fires → marriage broad window opens
Transit rule fires → window becomes active
Fast trigger rule fires → exact timing is narrowed down
Decision layer → outputs BROAD_WINDOW, NARROW_WINDOW, or TARGET_DAY
Best practice

Keep these as:

neutral
domain-scoped
versioned
calibration-friendly

That way the same structure can be reused for:

childbirth
job switch
business launch
relocation
surgery
retreat
exam timing
